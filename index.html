Interesting facts about camels:

  • Camels have humps to store water and fats.
  • Camels can breakdown the fats in the hump to get water and food when they are scarce.

Interesting facts about chameleons:

  • Chameleons can change their colour by their mood but can also use it to match it to its surroundings.
  • Chameleons can change colours because of specialcells called chromatophores which change colour based on size.

Interesting facts about Teddy bears:

  • Teddy bears are stuffed toys in shape of a bear.
  • The worlds smallest teddy bear is 0.7366 cm in length.

Interesting facts about pigeons:

  • A verity of pigeons call pink pigeons are only found in the island of mauritius.

Interesting facts about flying cicadas:

  • Cicadas are insects which produse courtship song with the help of special structure in their abdomen.
  • Cicadas can be large or small ranging from 2 to 5 cm in size.

Interesting facts about pandas :

  • Pandas have really thick fur which helps the to protect from fall if the fall from a height.
  • There are only 1600 giant pandas in the wild.
